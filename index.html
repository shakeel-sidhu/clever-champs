What is S.T.E.M.?

We are committed to growing a STEM generation through our books.

S

Science

S stands for Science which is is the study of the natural world, including physics, chemistry, and biology (just to name a few!)

T

Technology

T stands for Technology which is any innovation or device created for the purpose of meeting a human desire (ranging from a paper clip to a computer!)

E

Engineering

E stands for Engineering which is the glue that brings science, math, and technology together towards a workable solution

M

Mathematics

M stands for Mathematics which is the ability to analyze, reason, and interpret solutions to problems in a variety of real-world scenarios
